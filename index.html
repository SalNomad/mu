Welcome to Mutafuq

Mutafuq is a student service company that helps parents and students find academics to help them to find the correct path for their academic study and helps them to apply for academic courses, apply for English language courses, pre-master, and academic courses as well. Plus, provide tutors for various academic subjects. We tutor those in Primary, GCSE, A-Level and Special educational needs (SEN) such as Dyslexia.

We have a number of academics and experts in different areas including qualified teachers, and experienced academic tutors experts in teaching undergraduates and postgraduates taught courses. Many of our experts are experienced at providing support for students who require additional support, especially international students who are new to the UK education system. Our company service will support your learning to provide the right study path that is perfect for you.

You can find a tutor in less than 5 minutes by either visiting our website and searching for tutors in your major area, or you can phone our 1-2-1 support service which can talk you through how it all works and any specific needs you may have.

Uniqueness / Benefits

Mutafuq Educational Service Ltd provides education services to establish an effective relationship with universities.

Our services will start with our connections in the middle-east (Kuwait, Saudi Arabia), North Africa, and China. Our connections can do online advertising for Open-days and courses, registration, and provide information and help students with applying for the suitable courses they are interested in. Then we offer some guidance and induction for prospective students, especially international students.

Also, help them to find the right accommodation, using transport. For some students who are unfamiliar with the UK education system, we could provide a good induction about this as well.
